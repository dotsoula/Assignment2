    Pharmacological Action

    Aquamal QS comprises a fixed combination of 1 part of Artemether and 6 parts of Lumefantrine


    Absorption

    Artemether is absorbed quickly with peak plasma concentrations reaching in about 2 hours after dosing.


    Distribution

    Artemether and Lumefantrine are both highly bound to human serum proteins in vitro.


    Metabolism

    Artemether is rapidly and extensively metabolized in human liver microsomes to the biological active main metabolite.


    Elimination

    Artemether is rapidly cleared from plasma with an elimination haif-life of about 2-3 hours


    Therapeutic Indication

    Artemether/Lumefantrine is indicated for the treatment of uncomplicated P.falciparum malaria including multi-drug resistant strains of P.falciparum.


    Dosage & Administration

    Patients with acute malaria are frequently averse to food.


    Side Effect

    Artemether/Lumefantrine is well tolerated and there is no drugs induced serious unwanted side effects.


    Over-Dosage

    Contra-Indication

    Artemether/Lumwfantrine is contra-indicated to thise patients which have a history of hypersensibility to Artemether/Lumefantrine.


    Precaution

    Pregnancy: There are no adequate data from the use of Artemether/Lumefantrine in pregnancy women.
